📍 Why Delhi Works

Delhi is not one mood. It shifts from imperial boulevards to dense bazaars, from Mughal tombs to modern temples, often within the same afternoon.

The city rewards travelers who mix categories instead of chasing only landmarks. A good day in Delhi usually combines architecture, food, and one lived-in neighborhood.

That is why this version of the site is split into focused pages: we can go deeper without burying everything in one long scroll.
