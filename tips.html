💡 Essential Information

The practical side of Delhi, simplified

A little planning changes the city dramatically. Timing, transport, clothing, hydration, and realistic pacing matter more here than most first-time visitors expect.

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers the most pleasant weather. Avoid peak summer when temperatures can become exhausting.

Getting Around

Delhi Metro is the fastest and most comfortable way to travel. Use ride apps for last-mile access or late-evening convenience.

What to Wear

Dress comfortably for walking. Religious sites require modest clothing, and a scarf is often useful.

Food Tips

Choose busy stalls, drink sealed bottled water, and do not overcommit on spice if you are easing in.

Safety Tips

Stay alert as you would in any large metro. Keep valuables secure and use official transport options.

Budget Planning

Delhi can be done cheaply or luxuriously. The easiest way to control costs is smart transport and selective market spending.
